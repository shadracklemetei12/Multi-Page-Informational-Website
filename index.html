Welcome to the World of Specialty Coffee

Specialty coffee isn't just your average morning wake-up call; it is a movement dedicated to quality, sustainability, and celebrating the unique flavor profiles of coffee beans from around the globe.

Understanding the Beans

While there are dozens of coffee species, two main varieties dominate the global market. Here is how they compare:

Comparison of Major Coffee Varieties
Bean Variety Growing Altitude Flavor Profile Caffeine Content
Arabica High (600–2,000+ meters) Sweet, acidic, complex flavors (fruity, floral) Lower (~1.2% - 1.5%)
Robusta Low (0–800 meters) Bitter, earthy, woody, dark chocolate notes Higher (~2.2% - 2.7%)
